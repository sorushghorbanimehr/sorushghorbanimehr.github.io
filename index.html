Hi! I'm Sorush Ghorbanimehr

I am a senior undergraduate student at the IAUP. My main research interests are deep learning and its applications in medical imaging, bioinformatics, natural language processing, and wearable devices. I want to broaden my knowledge and experience in mentioned areas by learning theoretical aspects and developing experimental sides of these methods in real-life problems. The majority of my experiences were on the practical side, and I enjoyed implementing and tuning DL and ML models. It's worth mentioning that I am an ML engineer in a tech company, and I have valuable experience in the ML industry. My research goal is to propose innovative applications to solve current problems in computer vision and NLP, focusing on medical applications.
